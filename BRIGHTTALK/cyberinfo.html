Cyberbullying Info

Cyberbullying is the use of devices, like phones or computers to harm, irritate, harrass a user. It takes place through social media and online games.

Types of Cyberbullying

💬

Hurtful Messages

Sending mean or threating messages through chat or social media.

🚫

Exclusion

Deliberately leaving someone out of group chats or online activites.

📷

Sharing without permission

Posting photos or videos of someone without their consent.

🎭

Impersonation

Pretending to be someone else online to cause embarrassment or harm.

What to do?

1

Don't reply. Responding can make it worse. Take a screenshot first.

2

Tell a trusted adult - a parent, teacher, or school counsellor

3

Report and Block the person on the platform where it happened.

4

Save the Evidence - screenshots can help adults take action.

5

Remember it is never your fault. You deserve kindness.

Need help? Talk to someone 💙

  • TeenCare - Bullying - advice on bullying.
  • ChildLine - Free support for young persons.
  • Talk to a parent, teacher, or trusted adult.
