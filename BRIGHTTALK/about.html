About the Creator

Anastacia Low is a primary school student from Trinidad and Tobago. She crated this website as a part of the CARIRI Innovation Nation Power Up 2026 Competition

Why was BrightTalk made?

BrightTalk was created to raise awareness about Cyberbullying and to give kids a safe, kind space to share things they love online - without fear of mean comments or being targeted

💛 BrightTalk believes every child deserves a kind corner of the internet.

Who is it for?

BrightTalk is designed for kids ages 9-14 who want to share their art, books, hobbies and ideas in a friendly, welcoming community where kindness is the rule - not the exception

How does it stay safe?

  • 🏷️ Kids use fun, anonymous user names - no real names shown
  • 💬 A kindness prompt appears before every comment is posted
  • 🚩 Every post has a report button so unkind content can be flagged
  • 📃 Clear community rules are shown on the homepage.
